About O.B. Montessori

O.B. Montessori is a school founded on Montessori teaching. We focus on independence, hands-on learning, and helping students grow at their own pace. The school has been around for decades and continues to guide learners in a calm and structured environment.

Our History

1966 – The Beginning

The school first started with a simple goal: bring Montessori education to more young learners.

1980s – Growing Bigger

More levels and classrooms were added to support students as they continued learning.

Today – Modern Learning

Classrooms and programs now support modern learning while keeping Montessori values.
